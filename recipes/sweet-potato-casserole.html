Sweet Potato Casserole

Description:

This sweet potato casserole is a delicious and comforting dish that combines creamy mashed sweet potatoes

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups mashed sweet potatoes
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup mini marshmallows (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mashed sweet potatoes, granulated sugar, milk, eggs, vanilla extract, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Mix until smooth and well combined.
  3. Pour the sweet potato mixture into a greased 9x13 inch baking dish.
  4. If desired, sprinkle mini marshmallows evenly over the top of the casserole.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the casserole is set and the marshmallows are golden brown.
  6. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly before serving. Enjoy!
