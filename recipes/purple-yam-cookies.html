Purple Yam Cookies

Description:

These purple yam cookies are a delightful twist on traditional cookies, featuring the unique flavor and vibrant color of purple yams.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup mashed purple yams

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla extract, and mix until well combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, cinnamon, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  6. Fold in the mashed purple yams until evenly distributed.
  7. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased baking sheets.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  9. Remove from the oven and let cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack. Enjoy!
