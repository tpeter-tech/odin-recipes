Candied Yams Casserole with Pecans

Description:

This candied yams casserole with pecans is a sweet and savory dish that combines tender yams with a rich, buttery glaze and crunchy pecans. It's the perfect side dish for any holiday meal or family gathering.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups sliced yams (sweet potatoes)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chopped pecans

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the melted butter, brown sugar, maple syrup, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Mix until well combined.
  3. Add the sliced yams to the bowl and toss them in the glaze until they are evenly coated.
  4. Transfer the glazed yams to a greased 9x13 inch baking dish and spread them out in an even layer.
  5. Sprinkle the chopped pecans evenly over the top of the yams.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the yams are tender and the pecans are toasted.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly before serving. Enjoy!
